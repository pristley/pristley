## 💡 PHILOSOPHY

> *"Systems-first thinking. Observability-driven design. Trade-offs over silver bullets."*

I believe in building AI systems that are:
- **Observable** - Measurable, monitorable, understandable
- **Defensive** - Graceful degradation, circuit breakers, fallbacks
- **Pragmatic** - Balancing latency, cost, and accuracy in production
- **Human-in-the-loop** - Augmenting human decision-making, not replacing it
